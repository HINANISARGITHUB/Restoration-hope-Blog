Toy Drive!!

100 Kids, 100 Toys: Restoring Joy This Christmas
Hosted by Restoration Hope and New Destiny Ministry Center

The holiday season should be a time of joy, warmth, and hope but for many families in our
region, it can be a time of stress and heartbreak. That’s why Restoration Hope is excited to
host our annual Christmas celebration: 100 Kids, 100 Toys.

This special event aims to bless 200 children and teens in our community by providing age appropriate Christmas gifts and, most importantly, love.
While we continue to provide for children from infancy through age 13, we are placing a special focus this year on teens ages 12–18, a group often left out of traditional holiday outreach.

Our goal is to let them know: you are seen, valued, and celebrated.

Gifts for teens will include fun items like games and accessories, as well as practical
essentials such as hygiene products, gift cards, and inspirational items that boost self-esteem.
Families attending will enjoy a joyful play by New Destiny Ministry Center, light refresments and gifts.
